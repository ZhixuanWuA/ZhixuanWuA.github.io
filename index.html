👋 Hello! I'm Zhixuan.

I am a PhD candidate at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, advised by Prof. Cheng Bo. I am also a visiting research fellow at Nanyang Technological University, working with Assoc. Prof. Soujanya Poria.

My research lies at the intersection of machine vision, multi-modal understanding, and interactive cognition, with a focus on building AI systems that can perceive, reason, and interact in complex environments.
